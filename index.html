About Us

We are a painting and decorating services company covering all of Sydney. We pay special attention to detail in all our work. We use only skilled tradespeople and use only the best products on the market. We maintain the highest standards of quality and safety.
Ahoora Painting and Decorating are artists not just painting contractors and try to use creativity and professional techniques of painting and decorating. We are experts in employing efficient work methods to reduce the cost to you, our valued customers.
